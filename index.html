About Us

ModCtrl is an independent control panel building service based in Donegal, Ireland. We specialise in the design and support of small modular systems for energy monitoring, automation, and data integration. Focused on simplicity and reliability, we help businesses adopt the right hardware and protocols — whether it’s Modbus, BACnet, or custom gateways — without vendor lock-in. Our goal is to empower engineers and building managers with smarter, easier-to-integrate control solutions.

Services

• Hardware selection and consulting for control panels
• Modbus, BACnet, and gateway configuration support
• Pre-commissioning assistance and diagnostics
• Integration with third-party monitoring platforms
• Technical documentation and wiring diagrams
• Ongoing support for scalable modular systems
