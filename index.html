About Us

ModCtrl.ie is an independent panel builder based in Donegal, specializing in small modular control systems. We design, build, and support tailored control panels that integrate seamlessly into your projects, offering flexible, reliable solutions for energy monitoring, automation, and more. Our expertise lies in combining robust hardware with smart software solutions, including Modbus gateways and monitoring systems, to empower businesses and institutions to manage their energy and controls efficiently.

Services

  • Consultancy on hardware selection for custom control panels
  • Design and assembly of modular control panels for energy monitoring
  • Integration and support of Modbus devices and gateways
  • Pre-commissioning support and testing
  • Remote monitoring setup and troubleshooting
