About Us

ModCtrl.ie is an independent control panel consultancy based in Donegal. We design and support compact modular control systems for schools, offices, and energy-focused projects. Our services revolve around Modbus and BACnet integration, hardware consultancy, and practical support during the pre-commissioning phase. Our mission is to deliver sleek, efficient panels that just work — no fluff, no fuss.
